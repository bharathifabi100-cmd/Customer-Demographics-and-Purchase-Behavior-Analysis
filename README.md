# 📊 Customer Demographics & Purchase Behavior Analysis

# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **customer demographics and purchase behavior** using **Microsoft Excel** to identify spending patterns, customer segments, regional performance, and product trends.

The project demonstrates practical **Business Analytics** and **Data Analysis** skills through Pivot Tables, Charts, Conditional Formatting, and Business Insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The goal is to help businesses answer questions such as:

✅ Which customers spend the most?

✅ Which region generates the highest revenue?

✅ Which products perform the best?

✅ Who are the repeat customers?

✅ How do purchasing trends change over time?

# 📌 Key Insights

### 👴 Age Analysis

- Customers aged **60–70** spend the most.
- Customers aged **10–19** contribute the least.

---

### 🏠 Product Performance

- Home & Kitchen generates the highest revenue.
- Toys have fewer purchases but higher average value.
- Books show median spending performance.

---

### 🌎 Regional Analysis

- North has the largest customer base.
- East generates the highest monthly revenue.
- South shows consistent performance.

---

### 📅 Monthly Trends

- October records peak sales.
- December 2024 shows a significant decline.

---

### 🔁 Customer Behaviour

- 11% of customers are repeat buyers.
- High-value customers contribute significantly to total revenue.

---
